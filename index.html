Crystal Skull Collector

At the start of each game, each skull above is given a specific amount of points. When the skull is clicked, that amount will be added to the total score. The number below will be randomly generated. To win, match the total score to the random number. If the total score goes beyond the random number, the game is over. The amount of points of each of the skulls will change when a new game begins.
